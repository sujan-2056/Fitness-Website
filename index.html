The Club Fitness Difference

INCLUSIVE

At Club Fitness, our gym doors are open to everyone from all walks of life. No matter their shape, size, age, or experience level, we are dedicated to helping each person reach their personal health and fitness goals.

COMMITTED

For over 35 years, Club Fitness has been committed to the success of every BODY we serve. We are progressive and forward-thinking, constantly adapting to the evolving health and fitness needs of our members.

ACCESSIBLE

We make fitness totally accessible to everybody and every BODY type. We are the only St. Louis employee-owned gym with more locations and more amenities than anyone else.

VALUE

We offer more locations, more amenities, more classes, and more equipment with gym membership options that can fit any budget.

EMPLOYEE OWNED

Club Fitness has been an employee-owned gym since 2015. As owners, our team members take a lot of pride in providing the best possible gym experience for our members. Clean, functional, and friendly are the baseline standards our team members strive for every day.

LOCALLY OWNED

We opened our first location in St. Louis in 1983. Ever since then, Club Fitness has been committed to lifting its members to healthier, happier lives through fitness. We care about St. Louis because we are from St. Louis. We are proud to #LiftLocal!
